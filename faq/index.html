FAQ

What is Abstainers League?

Abstainers League is a youth movement promoting abstinence, moral discipline, and personal development through outreach programs and empowerment initiatives.

What is Abstinence?

Abstinence is the deliberate decision to avoid specific behaviours, especially those considered pleasurable, harmful, or morally inappropriate.

Who is an Abstainer?

Abstainers are individuals who deliberately choose not to engage in behaviours that violate social norms, such as premarital sex, cultism, drug abuse, internet fraud, and exam malpractice.

Can I abstain from sex until marriage?

YES! God has helped many young people through the Abstainers' League to remain sexually pure until marriage. This organization is committed to helping you attain and maintain sexual purity.

Is it okay to have a boyfriend/girlfriend in secondary school?

Genuine friendship develops naturally and doesn’t require a formal approach. When a boy or girl formally asks the opposite sex for friendship, it often implies courtship, which is meant for marriage. At the secondary school level, students should focus on academics. Therefore, having a boyfriend or girlfriend is NOT ALLOWED.

Who can join Abstainers League?

Anyone passionate about promoting positive values, especially youths aged 15-35, is welcome to join as a member or volunteer.

How can I become a volunteer?

Fill out the volunteer form on our website and you’ll be contacted for orientation and placement in suitable programs.

What programs does Abstainers League run?

We run outreach campaigns, mentorship programs, youth empowerment sessions, school workshops, and answer youth-focused questions through GRA (Get Right Answers).

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is free. However, we encourage voluntary donations to support programs and activities.

How can I support Abstainers League?

You can support by volunteering, spreading awareness, donating, or collaborating on community projects and campaigns.
